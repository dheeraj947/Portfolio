Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

Standard projects are delivered within 3-5 business days. We also offer 24-hour express delivery for urgent projects at a premium rate.

What file formats do you work with?

We accept all common video formats (MP4, MOV, AVI, etc.) and deliver in your preferred format, typically MP4 (H.264) for optimal quality and file size.

What's your revision policy?

We include 2 rounds of revisions with every project. Additional revisions are available at a nominal fee to cover our editors' time.

How do you structure pricing?

We price projects based on video length, complexity, and turnaround time. Contact us with your project details for a customized quote.

Do you provide source files?

Yes, we can provide project files (Premiere Pro, After Effects) for an additional fee. This allows you to make minor tweaks yourself in the future.
